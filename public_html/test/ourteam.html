STAFF

Carol Lamberg, Executive Director, has over 30 years of experience as a housing professional. She has worked for 12 years in a private consulting firm acting as a lobbyist and a financial advisor for various projects. She was a consultant to Settlement Housing Fund from itsousing Conference and is Regional Vice President of the National Housing Conference.

Susan Cole, Associate Director, joined Settlement Housing Fund in 1974. She has an MSW degree and a real estate brokers license. Her specialties are affirmative marketing, tenant slection and leadership training. Susan is involved wtih management, oversite of development, construction and the overall operation of projects. Shes is also responsible for tenant selection at all Settlement Housing Fund projects as well as many privately owned projects, including "80/20" buildings. She has trained home interviewers and social service staff at numerous sites, and she directed the leadership training program for the Housing Authority's ATP (Authority Transfer Program).

Lee Warshavsky, Assistant Director/General Counsel,came to Settlement Housing Fund in 1999. He holds a B.A. from Brown University and a J.D. from Boston University School of Law. Prior to joining Settlement Housing Fund he worked in several capacities at the New York City Department of Housing Preservation and Development for the Division of Housing Finance and Supportive Housing. At Settlement Housing Fund he is responsible for housing development and legal work.

Wendy Schorr, Assistant Director,came aboard Settlement Housing Fund in 2001. She has over 25 years of experience in the housing field and golds a Master's in City Planning from the University of Pennsylvania Wendy’s background is in operations, research and Wendy’s background is in operations, research and program development, and budgeting. She last worked at the New York City Department of Housing Preservation and Development and, prior to that, at the State of New York Mortgage Agency/New York State Housing Finance Agency.
