ST. JOHNS PLACE

St. John's Place Family Center, our homeless shelter in Crown Heights, Brooklyn, has helped more than 800 families make the transition into permanent housing in the nine years it has operated. Guided by its mission "Helping families achieve and sustain self sufficiency", St. John's Place is a comprehensive social services to 97 homeless families. Unlike many other shelters that restrict the admission of male spouses and children of various ages, St. John's Place has made a committment to serving whole families.

St. John's Place provides comprehensive child care services to 40 pre-school children. The program is licensed by the New York City Department of Health and is staffed by teachers specializing in early childhood education. A summer day camp provides additional opportunities for older children.

The professional staff at St. John's Place offer a myriad of skill-building programs, from life skills training to parenting classes, housing readiness, housing relocation assistance, day care, after school classes, and a summer camp with an intensive component for children.The Employment Center, which can serve 50 clients each year, has placed 43 students in non-subsidized jobs since 1997. The Employment Center offers comprehensive training in hard and soft job skills. The Center offers vocational skills classes in office administration, human services, and maintenance / janitorial services. Dress for success interviewing clothes are provided for students of the Center.

A health clinic operated by the New york Children's Health Project provides comprehensive medical services, with an on-site doctor and once a week visits from a dentist, for children and adults. A kids corner provides temporary infant care while St. John's Place builds its new infant care space.

The Career Resource Center enables students from the Employment Center to conduct an independent job search via traditional job postings, newspapers, and the internet. Staff members help students prepare for interviews by finding information on companies for which they will interview on the internet.

St. John's Place Family Center is committed to working with the community to improve Crown Heights. In conjunction with others in the neighborhood, they have created two community gardens, organized a coalition of service providers in Crown Heights, and host an annual neighborhood fair.

St John's Placehas established a strong working relationship with other social service agencies that provide substance abuse counseling, mental health counseling, health services youth mentoring, and legal assistance on site.
