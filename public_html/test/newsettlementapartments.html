Settlement Housing Fund, Inc.

Settlement Housing Fund is now embarking on a capital campaign for its New Settlement Community Campus on Jerome Avenue in the Bronx. The campaign will create a center for the neighborhood community that includes an indoor swimming pool, dance studios, and multi-purpose classroom spaces a block from its showcase housing development, New Settlement Apartments. Through a partnership with the New York City School Construction Authority, the center will adjoin a new public school for 1,100 students, pre-kindergarten through grade 12.





Settlement Housing Fund is responsible for the development of the school and the center. The campaign seeks funding specifically for the people, performing and studio arts, and other programs in a building that is annexed to the new school, which will be open year round, evenings, and weekends and managed by New Settlement Apartments and Settlement Housing Fund. Philanthropic contributions, along with public sector support and an allocation of New Market Tax Credits, will provide funding for the Settlement Housing Fund component of the larger project. The city is paying for the school. Beginning with strong participation from its Board of Directors and extended support from current and recent donors, Settlement Housing Fun seeks campaign gifts from individuals, families, businesses, corporations and foundations attracted to its accomplishments and model education programs.
